Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I start a new game?

A: On the main screen, tap "Start Game" to begin a new game.

Q: How do I change the theme or difficulty?

A: Use the pickers at the top of the game screen to select your preferred theme and difficulty.

Q: Where can I view my game results?

A: After completing a game, your results (score, time, flips, etc.) will be displayed on the results screen.
